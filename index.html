Chef Gerry Iannaccone makes his triumphant return to Branford alongside his life partner, Andrea “Drea” Coppola. Together, they return to their roots with Drea’s by Goodfellas—a dining experience that blends tradition, warmth, and hospitality. With a combined 50 years in the restaurant industry, Gerry and Andrea honor the Goodfellas legacy by presenting exquisite dishes prepared with simple, authentic ingredients and served with genuine care. From hand-crafted pastas and rich, traditional sauces to indulgent house-made desserts, every plate tells a story of heritage, love, and flavor. At Drea’s, we bring the heart of Italy to your table. Set in a warm, relaxed atmosphere, dining at Drea’s feels like a visit with Nonna—where stories are shared, laughter fills the air, and every meal becomes a memory. More than just a restaurant, Drea’s by Goodfellas is a place to gather, celebrate, and savor the timeless flavors of Italy.
